Cloudiness

The plot shows that the level of cloudiness is uniform for all latitudes. The creation of clouds is caused by water vapor in the atmosphere. Atmospheric temperatures decrease as altitude is increased. Accordingly, the temperature at high altitudes are approximately the same in terms of cloud formation.
