Max Temperature

Analyis of the temperature data shows that the temperature increases as the latitude approaches the equator (0 degrees).

As the Earth orbits the Sun, the Earth's temperature is affected. The Earth spins on its axis approximately 23.5 degrees. As a result the temperatue is not uniform. The plot illustrates that the equatorial regions are hotter than the polar regions. The Sun's radiation is more intense at the equatorial region.
